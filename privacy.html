Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect your personal information when you use our telemedicine services at drforamkotharishah.com.

When you use our services, we may collect the following information:

3. WhatsApp Communication

By booking a consultation, you consent to receive WhatsApp messages from Dr Foram Clinic including appointment confirmations, prescription details, medicine reminders and health tips. You may opt out at any time by messaging STOP.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your medical and personal information seriously. All data is stored securely and is accessible only to authorised medical staff. Payment information is encrypted and processed through Cashfree's secure payment gateway.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal or medical information with third parties except:

  • With your consent for referrals to specialists or laboratories

  • With secure payment processors for transaction processing

6. Cookies

Our website uses basic cookies to improve your browsing experience. No sensitive personal data is stored in cookies.
