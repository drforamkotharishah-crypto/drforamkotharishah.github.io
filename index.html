Compliance & Regulation

This platform operates in full accordance with NMC Telemedicine Practice Guidelines 2020. All consultations are conducted by Dr. Foram Kothari Shah, a registered physician (Maharashtra Medical Council). AI tools are used solely to assist the doctor in organising patient history and reports — all clinical decisions, diagnoses, and prescriptions are made exclusively by the doctor. Prescriptions are issued at the doctor's sole clinical discretion and are valid for a maximum of 3 days per NMC Guidelines. No Schedule H1, H, or X drugs are prescribed via telemedicine. Patients are advised to seek in-person emergency care if their condition is life-threatening. This platform does not replace in-person consultation for serious or complex conditions.
